~ Game Rules ~

  • 🏍️ Control bike with joystick
  • ⚠️ Avoid moving cars & chasing police
  • 💨 Game speeds up over time
  • 🔥 Difficulty increases with score
  • 💥 Accidents end the game!
  • 👮 Kick police when they are close, this will gain +50 score
  • 👊 Once you kicked, wait for cooldown period of 1 second to kick again
  • 🔊 Adjust game sounds using the volume controls
